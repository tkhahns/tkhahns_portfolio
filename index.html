Most recent work

Alex to the Rescue

Our project, Alex to the Rescue is about Alex the Bot - a simulation of a self-driven car. Our aim is to overcome a random map and try to reconstruct the map using Raspberry Pi OS and LiDAR. In addition, Alex was able to run through obstacles and identify various types of objects on the map, and finally park in a specified parking lot.

The A-maze-ing Race

Our project, the A-maze-ing Race is a maze which our robot has to clear in the shortest time. Along the way, there are various colour challenges which the robot would face and our robot - the mBot - needs to react differently according to the various colours it detects in order to complete the race.
